About Us

As siblings, we've dreamed of working on projects together for years. Our passion for technology and love for learning and diving into interesting projects has only grown as we've advanced in our careers. Individually, we're both visonary and value execution above all else, and together, we're unstoppable. With a background as scrappy entrepreneurs who have launched several businesses and even more side hustles, we know what it takes to launch new products and the impact that resources and expertise can have on early-stage companies. That's why we've come together to form PerchPond, a hybrid of a digital services agency, advisory and venture studio. Our goal is to help founders bring their visions to life and launch their products with confidence, and we're excited to be on this journey with you
